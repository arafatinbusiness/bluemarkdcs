Microphone Test Page

Click "Start Test" to begin microphone testing.

Available Audio Input Devices:

Instructions:

  1. Click "Start Test" - Chrome will ask for microphone permission
  2. Grant permission to use your microphone
  3. Speak into your microphone - you should see the visualizer move
  4. Click "List Devices" to see all detected audio input devices
  5. Look for "Ryzen HD Audio Controller Analog Stereo" or similar

Troubleshooting:

  • If no permission prompt appears, check Chrome settings: chrome://settings/content/microphone
  • If no devices appear, your microphone may not be detected by Chrome
  • Try closing other applications that might be using the microphone
  • Restart Chrome if issues persist
